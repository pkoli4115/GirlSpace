Data Deletion Instructions

GirlSpace

Effective date: 12 November 2025 • Last updated: 12 November 2025

At GirlSpace, we respect your right to control your data. If you’ve logged in using Google or Facebook and wish to delete your account or all associated data, follow the instructions below.

Option 1 — In-App Deletion (Recommended)

  1. Open the GirlSpace app.
  2. Go to Profile → Settings → Delete My Account.
  3. Confirm deletion. This will permanently remove your profile, messages, and media from our servers within 7 days.

Option 2 — Manual Request via Email

If you no longer have access to the app or wish to ensure deletion manually:

  1. Send an email to support@qtilabs.com.
  2. Use the subject line: Delete My GirlSpace Account.
  3. In the body, include the email address you used to log in (Google or Facebook) and optionally your user ID (if known).
  4. Our team will verify ownership and delete all associated data within 7 business days.

Data That Will Be Deleted

  • Your profile information (name, email, bio, and photo).
  • All chat messages, posts, and uploaded media.
  • Any analytics or usage data tied to your account.

Data That May Be Retained Temporarily

Some residual information (e.g., logs or backups) may remain for up to 30 days for security, legal, or backup purposes, after which it will be permanently removed.
